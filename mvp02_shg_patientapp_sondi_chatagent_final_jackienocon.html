Meet Sondi, the SHG Chat Agent

An AI assistant embedded in the existing SHG mobile app — no new app to install — that talks patients through the moments that create the most anxiety and the most repeat questions to staff.

What it's built for: Sondi closes the information gap patients feel while waiting — starting in the ED, where uncertainty is highest, but built as a hospital-wide capability across outpatient, inpatient, and allied health. It reads live status from the Flow Board (MVP 1) and shares only general, non-clinical info — queue position, wait estimates, result-ready status — in line with UAE PDPL. Anything clinical or urgent-sounding is handed straight to a human.
